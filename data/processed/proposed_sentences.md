# Proposed Sentences for Review (Unit 1~10)

`sentences_example.md`의 문법 형식을 기준으로 `data` 폴더에서 추출한 검토용 문장 리스트입니다. (각 Unit별 3문장씩)

## [Unit 1~5]

### Unit 1: 관계대명사 what
1. English: "The judge was impressed by **what** she said and finally gave her permission." (정사열 L04-11)
2. English: "That was **what** impressed me most in the movie." (정사열 L04-12)
3. English: "That’s **what** I need to learn from her." (정사열 L04-15)

### Unit 2: 현재완료 진행형
1. English: "Jake **has been sleeping** all afternoon." (이재영 L02-158)
2. English: "I **have been waiting** for his call for two hours." (이재영 L03-82)
3. English: "I **have been learning** English since I was 10." (이재영 L03-83)

### Unit 3: to부정사 의미상주어
1. English: "It is important for everyone **to value** the resources." (이재영 L06-29)
2. English: "It is important for us **to protect** nature." (이재영 L06-01)
3. English: "It is necessary for us **to reduce** the amount of waste." (이재영 L06-02)

### Unit 4: 과거 완료
1. English: "I was glad my dad **had made** me wear gloves." (정사열 L02-35)
2. English: "He couldn’t open the door because he **had lost** his key." (정사열 L02-16)
3. English: "Malala Yousafzai won the Nobel Prize because she **had fought** for children’s rights." (정사열 L03-19)

### Unit 5: 명사 수식 분사
1. English: "The man **sitting** under the tree is my uncle." (이재영 L02-03)
2. English: "The **broken** chair was fixed by my dad." (이재영 L02-02)
3. English: "The girl **reading** a book in the corner is Nari." (이재영 L02-05)

## [Unit 6~10]

### Unit 6: 분사구문
1. English: "**Hearing** this, I felt really sorry for her." (정사열 L04-09)
2. English: "**Watching** this movie, I could learn how to face challenges in life." (정사열 L04-16)
3. English: "**Walking** on the street, he looked at the map." (정사열 L04-17)

### Unit 7: 명사절 if / whether
1. English: "You also have to check **if** they are based on facts." (정사열 L07-P-03)
2. English: "I don’t know **if** it will rain tomorrow." (이재영 L04-01)
3. English: "I’m curious **whether** she will come to the party." (이재영 L04-04)

### Unit 8: too ~ to
1. English: "If a story or a photo seems **too** good **to** be true, stop and think." (정사열 L07-14)
2. English: "Science is **too** difficult **for me to finish** my homework." (정사열 L08-05)
3. English: "No, it’s **too** difficult **for me to make** food." (정사열 L08-90)

### Unit 9: 가정법 과거
1. English: "**If** I **had** wings, I **would fly** in the sky." (정사열 L05-18)
2. English: "**If** I **got** a letter from you, I **would be** very happy." (정사열 L05-08)
3. English: "I **would be** so happy **if** you **were** with me." (정사열 L05-14)

### Unit 10: so ~ that 구문
1. English: "The PCI lost **so** many seats **that** it almost seems dead." (정사열 L05-38)
2. English: "The street food was **so** cheap and delicious **that** workers ate it." (이재영 L04-132)
3. English: "Dorothy was **so** good at that she was invited to join a men’s team." (이재영 L08-113)
