Many of the volcanoes on Earth

and it is home to around 75% of the
world’s active volcanoes. Indonesia
alone has over 120 active volcanoes
since the country lies on the Ring of
Fire. Th  is ring is also the area where

What volcanoes do is not always
bad for humans, however. The soil
near a volcano is good for farming.
Volcanoes are also a great tourist
attraction. Looking for fantastic

Halla in Jejudo, Mt. Etna in Sicily, or
Diamond Head in Hawaii every year.
Volcanoes can be used to produce
electricity, too. Iceland, for example,
actively turns its volcanoes’ heat
energy into electricity. Today people
around the world are studying ways
to live in greater harmony with
volcanoes, and they are hopeful that
more ways will be found soon.

destroyed, and air traffic can be
threatened. Volcanoes can also have
serious effects on world weather. A
volcanic eruption shoots lots of ash
and dust into the air, preventing
some of the sunlight from reaching

weather patterns.
